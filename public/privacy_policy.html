Privacy Policy

LeSixDegrees does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

What We Store

We store your game progress (selected players and performance) locally in your browser using localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by the website owner.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with the site. All data is anonymized and no personally identifiable information is collected. Analytics will only be enabled if the user consents to cookies. If cookies are declined, no analytics data is collected or transmitted.

IP Addresses

Google Analytics may collect users' IP addresses by default as part of traffic analysis. However, IP anonymization is explicitly enabled in our configuration to ensure that full IP addresses are never stored or processed. This is done to comply with privacy regulations like GDPR and CCPA.

Cookies

This site uses cookies only for analytics purposes. A cookie consent banner is displayed on first visit, and analytics cookies are only set if the user accepts. Refusing cookies disables all analytics tracking.

Your Rights

You have the right to request deletion of your analytics data and opt out of tracking. Contact us at [your@email.com] for any requests.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Analytics to collect information about how visitors use this Website. This includes details such as your IP address, which is anonymized before being stored on Google's servers in the United States. IP anonymization means that a portion of your IP address is masked, preventing it from being used to precisely identify your location or device. Google uses this anonymized data to evaluate website traffic, generate reports for us, and provide other related services. In some cases, Google may transfer this information to third parties if legally required or if those parties process the data on Google’s behalf. You can disable cookies by adjusting your browser settings. By continuing to use this Website and accepting cookies, you agree to the collection and processing of your data by Google as described. For more control, you can opt out using the tools provided at https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout/ .

Children's Privacy

LeSixDegrees is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from minors.
